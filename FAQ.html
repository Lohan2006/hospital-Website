Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our hospital and services.

What are the hospital's visiting hours?

Our visiting hours are from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM every day.

How do I make an appointment with a doctor?

You can call our hospital reception at 123-456-7890 or use our online appointment system.

Does the hospital accept insurance?

Yes, we accept a wide range of insurance providers. Please check with our billing department for more details.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Bring a valid ID, insurance information, and any relevant medical records.

Where is the pharmacy located?

The pharmacy is located on the ground floor, near the main entrance.
